Brian Dunlap:

A Brief History

I like to make jokes and I love to write, and the marriage of the two often leads to long, absurd narratives with absolutely no bearing in reality (interesting factoid: Marie Antoinette maniacally collected ivory dog statues, which ironically led to her death).

  • May 18, 1989

    A Human Is Born

    Just as Brian is born, a butterfly perches squarely on his nose. An auspicious sign, and according to ancient Nordic mythos the mark of a born programmer.

  • May 2011

    Event Horizon

    A bona fied Russian major with a Bachelor of Arts under his belt is thrust into the world. Brian quickly realizes that college majors and career trajectories don't always align.

  • December 2014

    A Fateful Encounter

    Working at a marketing agency, Brian sleuthes into the dev team's camp and brushes shoulders with JavaScript and Backbone. He feels fear, but also intrigue. Important seeds are sewn.

  • August 2015

    Renaissance

    Brian takes a leap of faith, making a 180-degree career pivot, and enrolls in The Iron Yard in Atlanta. The seeds of passion burst into life, and professional doubt is subsumed by a new, vigorious love for finding new ways to build the things he's always wanted.
